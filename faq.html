Frequently Asked Questions

What age ranges is Party Paradise appropriate for?

We would recommend only parties involving people below 20 years old; we do teenage parties too for example paintball parties or horror parties.

As well as younger children's parties like Disney princess parties or race car parties.

What is the maximum and minimum number of people at your parties?

We do parties for any number of people, be it 1000 people for the incredibly extroverted child or be it one for that incredibly lonely kid.

What if a child does not like the party you throw them?

Then he or she will be shot. Sorry, just my Wei-ish sense of humour. The party will be totally refunded.

What if either the child whom your party is being thrown for or one of the guests injure themselves at a party of yours?

As your children are our top priority, in this disastrous scenario, the party will have to be put on hold while the catastrophe is sorted out.

Related question: What if someone at the party injurs you?

I don't think any child will be able to injure me.
